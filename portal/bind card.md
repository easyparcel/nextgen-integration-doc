# How add bind card?
Howdy people! Do you know that by adding a debit or credit cards on your EasyParcel account.

Moreover, register a debit/credit card is a MUST to kick start an international delivery. It’s simple, here’s how you can do it by simply log in to your EasyParcel account:

## 3 Simple Steps to Add Debit/Credit Cards on your EasyParcel account!
**Step 1:** From the sidebar of the [dashboard](https://app.easyparcel.com/dashboard), click on the **“Wallet”** and then select **“Bank & Cards“**.
<img width="1280" height="800" alt="Screenshot 2026-03-25 at 11 50 24 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d30a0dee-c423-43a3-8bda-22b10239233c" />

**Step 2:** Insert the Billing Address. Once done, click the **"go to Bind Card"**.
<img width="1280" height="800" alt="Screenshot 2026-03-25 at 11 51 56 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/130c71de-543f-401c-bfe5-c1ea426141b2" />

**Step 3:** Enter your card details. Make sure the details are correct to avoid card errors. Click **“Pay MYR 1.00”** to finish.
<img width="1280" height="800" alt="Screenshot 2026-03-25 at 11 52 27 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/3b90f7b3-2452-4d32-9e57-9baee4961fc9" />

Now, you have successfully bind your card in your EasyParcel account.
