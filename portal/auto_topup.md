# How to enable auto Topup in your EasyParcel Account?
Howdy people! 👋 Do you know that you can enable Auto Topup in your EasyParcel account so you never have to worry about running out of credit?

Once Auto Topup is enabled, your wallet will be automatically topped up whenever your balance falls below the threshold you set. It’s simple and convenient—here’s how it works:

# Why You Should Enable Auto Topup:

- **Never Run Out of Credit:**
Your wallet will automatically top up when it drops below your set threshold, so your shipping process won’t be interrupted.

- **Smooth Order Processing:**
Avoid failed bookings or delays due to insufficient wallet balance.

- **Same Benefits as Manual Topup:**
You still enjoy the same rewards, credits, and coupons from your selected topup package.

- **Fully Automated Convenience:**
No need to manually top up every time—everything runs automatically in the background.

- **Email Notification:**
You will receive an email notification every time an auto topup is successfully triggered.

# Steps to Enable Auto Topup on your EasyParcel account:
**Step 1:** From the sidebar of the [dashboard](https://app.easyparcel.com/dashboard), click on the **“Credit”** and then select **“Auto Topup“**.
<img alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4e0d2936-1e40-4d0d-8fb1-a6f9e12d2148" />
**Step 2:** Then, click the **"Edit"**.
<img alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/bcc3436c-f275-41d5-9cbb-45db8f95d249" />
**Step 3:** Enable the **"Auto Topup"** setting, it will turn green.
<img alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/015ca8c1-a2e3-4e69-aff7-9165d53bf95d" />
**Step 4:** Remember set the amount of **"Auto topup when credit balance fall below:"** field.Makesure the amount between RM 2 and RM 10000.
<img alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/23176e96-c543-49d3-a967-99bcffc5231c" />
**Step 5:** Select the **"Topup Package"** that you prefer.
<img alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/06985ef7-5b38-4dbc-9ac3-fea1cfbe9d6e" />
**Step 6:** If your account **doesn’t bind card**, you are required to **"Add New Card"** at here.
<img alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/aff48e45-64cc-4b71-800d-82571b944489" />
**Step 7:** Enter your card details. Make sure the details are correct to avoid card errors. Remember click **"Bind Card button"** after you have finished filling in the details.
<img alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/fadae7c4-cf01-4588-9e74-c42b04028f20" />
**Step 8:** Click **"Save changes"** button.
<img alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1e70f724-73d4-4dda-a13e-68d1f1cf5d36" />

Now, you have successfully enabled **Auto Topup** in your EasyParcel account.
