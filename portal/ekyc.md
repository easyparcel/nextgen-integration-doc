# How do I verify my account (eKYIC)?
## Quick Summary
To verify your EasyParcel account (eKYC), scan the QR code displayed on the screen with your phone, log in, and click "Let's Get Started". Upload clear photos of your NRIC (front and back), confirm the auto-filled details, and submit a, clear, well-framed selfie. Ensure your documents are not blurry and the details match your ID to avoid rejection.

## Why do I need to verify?
There are so many features you can enjoy when you verify your account. EasyParcel is always innovating our platform to serve you for many years to come. And for more innovations to enjoy, you need to verify your account for security purposes. Learn more about [what account verification](https://helpcentre-my.easyparcel.com/support/solutions/articles/9000226619-what-is-account-verification-ekyc-and-what-does-this-mean-for-you-) is to help you understand it better.

## How to verify my account (eKYIC)?
**Step 1:** When you're required to verify your account, a QR code will pop up, which you'll need to scan with your phone to proceed to verification 🤳.

👉 <a href="https://app.easyparcel.com/account/verification/qr" target="_blank">Click here to Start</a> 👈

**📋 Notes for Laptop user:** this when you click 👆 in your laptop, you will get the QR code which need to scan with your phone to proceed to verification.

**Step 2:** You will be prompted to log into your account.
![Login](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d0e0ab85-230b-4e0f-b6e3-b3f68f172306)

**Step 3:** Once logged in, you may read the reminder given, then click on "Let's Get Started".
![lets start](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c2d4e89e-4bc7-4ea7-8b1d-d84975d5feb4)

**Step 4:** Click on the button to start taking photos of your NRIC (front and back).

**Note:** Make sure the photos are clear and do not capture any lighting on them.
![Verify IC](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6a73f6ab-c5aa-421a-9944-c3bd80530237)

**Step 5:** Take note of the information displayed and ensure everything is correct before clicking "Confirm".
![detail](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5e40a2d7-d651-4c84-9f8a-7cbdc34d092a)

**Step 6:** It's time to take a selfie, and make sure to avoid flash lighting and that your photo is in the frame. Then click "Submit Photo"
![Selfie](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5baed51f-8e9f-40a7-a5ca-84d1a06d5d91)

**Step 7:** Yayy! Your account has been verified, you can now click "Proceed" and it will take you back to your account.
