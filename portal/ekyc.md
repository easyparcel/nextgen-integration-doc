# How do I verify my account (eKYIC)?
## Quick Summary
To verify your EasyParcel account (eKYC), scan the QR code displayed on the screen with your phone, log in, and click "Let's Get Started". Upload clear photos of your NRIC (front and back), confirm the auto-filled details, and submit a, clear, well-framed selfie. Ensure your documents are not blurry and the details match your ID to avoid rejection.

## Why do I need to verify?
There are so many features you can enjoy when you verify your account. EasyParcel is always innovating our platform to serve you for many years to come. And for more innovations to enjoy, you need to verify your account for security purposes. Learn more about [what account verification](https://helpcentre-my.easyparcel.com/support/solutions/articles/9000226619-what-is-account-verification-ekyc-and-what-does-this-mean-for-you-) is to help you understand it better.

## How to verify my account (eKYIC)?
**Step 1:** When you're required to verify your account, a QR code will pop up, which you'll need to scan with your phone to proceed to verification 🤳.

👉 <a href="https://app.easyparcel.com/account/verification/qr" target="_blank">Click here to Start</a> 👈
