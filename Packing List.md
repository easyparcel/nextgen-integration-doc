# Packing List on the AWB (Import Version Integration only)
To enable the AWB to include the **packing list** as shown in the image below, please refer to the attached file for guidance.
<img width="1280" height="602" alt="Screenshot 2026-02-27 at 2 43 14 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/46434bbd-7ebd-42ef-a7a4-39d09f3a02e9" />

---
## Please take note that you can **ignore the e-commerce platform shown in the image** you only need to follow the steps to enable the packing list in your AWB.

First, Log in to your EasyParcel account -> **EasyConnect** -> **Installed E-Commerce Apps** -> Find your E-commerce Store -> **open APP**
<img width="1280" height="652" alt="Screenshot 2026-02-27 at 2 35 49 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1219f925-da7a-441e-ac65-365ae0a62381" />

After that, open the **Settings** -> Find the **enable packing list on AWB** -> Change it into **"Yes"** -> Click the update button.
<img width="1017" height="524" alt="Screenshot 2026-02-27 at 2 42 17 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ec78e5ef-bbf4-497a-8920-1ae09aa9883a" />

Switch to **Order** 
<img width="1010" height="541" alt="Screenshot 2026-02-27 at 2 38 58 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/eeb875e8-48bb-456c-8438-35960a46f748" />

Select the order that have been fulfilled, then press the **Download AWB** button.
<img width="970" height="425" alt="Screenshot 2026-02-27 at 2 40 33 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b501464a-5a4e-4c73-b818-bb8e55caa084" />
****Take Note:** Do not click the printer button or the Tracking number, both of this button wont include the packing list on your AWB.

---

Lastly, click **“here”** and the AWB will be downloaded to your device. You may then open the file to check your AWB.
