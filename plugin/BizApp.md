# EasyParcel BizApp NextGen Integration – Plugin Version

#### Step 1: Login to your EasyParcel account.

#### Step 2: Click **EasyConnect**.
![EasyParcel BizApp Step 1](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/step1.png)

#### Step 3: Click **Add Ecommerce App**. Search for **BizApp**, then click **Install App**.
![EasyParcel BizApp Step 2](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/ba2.png)

#### Step 4: Fill in the **Store name** and **Store URL**, then click **Add**.
![EasyParcel BizApp Step 3](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/ba3.png)

#### Step 5: Your store API key / Integration ID will be generated. Copy it to integrate with your store.
![EasyParcel BizApp Step 4](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/ba4.png)

#### Step 6: Click “Profile & Tetapan”.
![EasyParcel BizApp Step 5](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/ba13.png)

#### Step 7: Fill in necessary details such as API key and pick up address.
![EasyParcel BizApp Step 6](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/ba5.png)

#### Step 8: Click “Kemaskini Profil”.
![EasyParcel BizApp Step 7](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/ba6.png)

#### Step 9: Click ” Hantar Record Ke EasyParcel”.
![EasyParcel BizApp Step 8](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/ba7.png)

#### Step 10: Select the pickup date and type of parcel before check out.
![EasyParcel BizApp Step 9](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/ba8.png)

#### Step 11: Check your EasyParcel credit Balance.
![EasyParcel BizApp Step 10](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/ba9.png)

#### Step 12: Select your preferred courier and click “Hantar Ke EasyParcel”.
![EasyParcel BizApp Step 11](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/ba10.png)

#### Step 13: Click” Ya” to confirm your order.
![EasyParcel BizApp Step 12](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/ba11.png)

#### Step 14: Click “Cetak Airway Bill” to print consignment note.
![EasyParcel BizApp Step 13](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/ba12.png)
