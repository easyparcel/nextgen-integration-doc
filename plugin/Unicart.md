# EasyParcel Unicart NextGen Integration – Plugin Version

#### Step 1: Login to your EasyParcel account.

#### Step 2: Click **EasyConnect**.
![EasyParcel 91App Step 1](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/step1.png)

#### Step 3: Click **Add Ecommerce App**. Search for **Unicart**, then click **Install App**.
<img width="1280" height="709" alt="unicart1" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/680ee916-bfa3-4264-80ea-5733e8d22ced" />

#### Step 4: Fill in the **Store name** and **Store URL**, then click **Add**.
<img width="1280" height="708" alt="unicart2" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/232d9d59-56e7-4b0f-b428-830c580422ec" />

#### Step 5: Your store API key / Integration ID will be generated. Copy it to integrate with your store.
<img width="1280" height="710" alt="unicart3" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ae0e980c-defe-4b61-9250-02ccff83edc4" />

#### Step 6: Go to Unicart’s EasyParcel page, paste it in Integration Key field. Fill up EasyParcel Email Address, and the address of Sender Collection.
<img width="726" height="400" alt="unicart4" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f311434f-6b91-4b23-8f31-2b0e8975381d" />

#### Step 7: After that, select the Shipping Rate to charge based on EasyParcel member rate or courier public rate.
##### Select the Weight Unit to calculate shipping fee accordingly. 
##### Select the Tax Class with SST @ 6% if your business is SST registered.
##### Select the Geo Zone to use EasyParcel service.
##### Select Default Shipping to sync orders to EasyParcel.
##### Enable Display Delivery Weight to show shipping weight at checkout.
##### Enable Pigeon Status if you offer self pick up service provided by Pigeon.
##### You can adjust the shipping fee by increasing, decreasing or remaining the actual charges.
##### May use Sorting to control the sequence of EasyParcel at checkout and select Customer Group that entitled for EasyParcel. (Optional)
##### Choose the Shipping Method to be displayed when checkout with Easyparcel at the webstore.
##### Enable Status to display EasyParcel option on your UniCart’s Checkout page.
<img width="586" height="563" alt="unicart5" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1af8f3c2-a5fc-46a4-8d93-2c5f3d61753c" />

#### Step 8: Fill in the API Key in order to get the courier service for “drop-off”
<img width="356" height="84" alt="unicart6" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/97cbcd80-34d3-41bd-a5cf-0332a8492311" />

#### Step 9: Click Install button.
<img width="83" height="43" alt="unicart7" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9babb0c9-2c41-4092-a77f-243ead616290" />

#### Step 10: Congratulations! You have successfully activated EasyParcel and customers can checkout with EasyParcel on your Unicart store.
<img width="1067" height="653" alt="unicart8" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4d58cb90-fee5-4b09-be92-9585031569cc" />
