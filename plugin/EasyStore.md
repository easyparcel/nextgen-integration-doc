# EasyParcel EasyStore NextGen Integration - Plugin Version

#### Step 1: Login to your EasyParcel account.

#### Step 2: Click 'EasyConnect'.
![EasyParcel EasyStore Step 1](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/step1.png)

#### Step 3: Click 'Add Ecommerce App'. Find the 'EasyStore'. After that, click 'Install App'.
![EasyParcel EasyStore Step 2](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/easystore2.png)

#### Step 4: Fill in the 'Store name' and 'Store URL'. After that, click 'Add'.
![EasyParcel EasyStore Step 3](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/easystore3.png)

#### Step 5: Your store API key/Integration ID will be generated. Copy it to integrate with your store.
![EasyParcel EasyStore Step 4](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/easystore4.png)

#### Step 6: Login to your EasyStore account.
![EasyParcel EasyStore Step 5](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/easystore5.png)

#### Step 7: Click 'Apps'.
![EasyParcel EasyStore Step 6](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/easystore6.png)

#### Step 8: Click 'Browse more apps'.
![EasyParcel EasyStore Step 7](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/easystore7.png)

#### Step 9: Click 'EasyParcel Malaysia'.
![EasyParcel EasyStore Step 8](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/easystore8.png)

#### Step 10: Click 'Install this app'.
![EasyParcel EasyStore Step 9](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/easystore9.png)

#### Step 11: Enter your API key (Integration ID) and origin address from which you ship the orders to the customer. Click 'Save'.
![EasyParcel EasyStore Step 10](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/easystore10.png)

#### Step 12: Click 'Installed apps' and make sure your EasyParcel Malaysia is 'Activated'.
![EasyParcel EasyStore Step 11](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/easystore11.png)
