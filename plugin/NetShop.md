# EasyParcel NetShop NextGen Integration – Plugin Version

#### Step 1: Login to your EasyParcel account.

#### Step 2: Click **EasyConnect**.
![EasyParcel NetShop Step 1](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/step1.png)

#### Step 3: Click **Add Ecommerce App**. Search for **SquareLet**, then click **Install App**.
![EasyParcel NetShop Step 2](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/nt2.png)

#### Step 4: Fill in the **Store name** and **Store URL**, then click **Add**.
![EasyParcel NetShop Step 3](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/nt3.png)

#### Step 5: Your store API key / Integration ID will be generated. Copy it to integrate with your store.
![EasyParcel NetShop Step 4](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/nt4.png)

#### Step 6: Login to your NetShop admin panel. Click on “Data Management” >”EasyParcel”.
![EasyParcel NetShop Step 5](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/ns5.png)

#### Step 7: Click on “EasyParcel API”.
![EasyParcel NetShop Step 6](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/ns6.png)

#### Step 8: Fill in the API Key at the destinated area.
![EasyParcel NetShop Step 7](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/ns7.png)

#### Step 9: Tick on the orders you would like to get delivery quotes from us and click “Get EasyParcel Quotes”.
![EasyParcel NetShop Step 8](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/ns8.png)

#### Step 10: Select your preferred courier.
![EasyParcel NetShop Step 9](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/ns9.png)

#### Step 11: Once you’ve done, tick on the checkbox and click “Submit To EasyParcel”.
![EasyParcel NetShop Step 10](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/ns10.png)
