# EasyParcel Wasep.me NextGen Integration - Plugin Version

#### Step 1: Login to your EasyParcel account.

#### Step 2: Click 'EasyConnect'.
![EasyParcel EasyStore Step 1](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/step1.png)

#### Step 3: Click 'Add Ecommerce App'. Find the 'Wasep.me'. After that, click 'Install App'.
<img width="1280" height="711" alt="wasep1" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a7bb4271-c372-4109-8f52-717cf8b49e47" />

#### Step 4: Fill in the 'Store name' and 'Store URL'. After that, click 'Add'.
<img width="1280" height="710" alt="wasep2" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/61a8747a-f34e-432f-a8e1-6d61d79fc069" />

#### Step 5: Your store API key/Integration ID will be generated. Copy it to integrate with your store.
<img width="1280" height="711" alt="wasep3" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0ff0ed4a-53ff-4543-9381-dd119530485a" />
