# EasyParcel Wasep.me NextGen Integration - Plugin Version

#### Step 1: Login to your EasyParcel account.

#### Step 2: Click 'EasyConnect'.
![EasyParcel EasyStore Step 1](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/step1.png)

#### Step 3: Click 'Add Ecommerce App'. Find the 'Wasep.me'. After that, click 'Install App'.
<img width="1280" height="711" alt="wasep1" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a7bb4271-c372-4109-8f52-717cf8b49e47" />

#### Step 4: Fill in the 'Store name' and 'Store URL'. After that, click 'Add'.
<img width="1280" height="710" alt="wasep2" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/61a8747a-f34e-432f-a8e1-6d61d79fc069" />

#### Step 5: Your store API key/Integration ID will be generated. Copy it to integrate with your store.
<img width="1280" height="711" alt="wasep3" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0ff0ed4a-53ff-4543-9381-dd119530485a" />

#### Step 6: Log in wasep.me. Click “Store” tab under “Maintenance”.
<img width="1151" height="565" alt="wasep4" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/337b5352-f789-45bb-8330-6933de5611ec" />

#### Step 7: Click “Premium”.
<img width="1151" height="565" alt="wasep5" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/36b3a754-2025-4ecd-98a3-e164ae70abf1" />

#### Step 8: Enter your API key(From EasyParcel) and select your preferrable settings, click the button “Save” below.
<img width="1151" height="565" alt="wasep6" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f51d71ac-e5df-4b06-b499-3ec1926478db" />

#### Step 9: When you’ve successfully saved the details, you will see the “Activate EasyParcel” tab.
<img width="1151" height="565" alt="wasep7" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/16241b99-983f-4770-9e33-00c11a087935" />

#### Step 10: In order to get Airwaybill for delivery, click “Orders” tab under “Actions”.
<img width="1151" height="565" alt="wasep8" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8b1e7b9a-6516-4416-a92d-9277c361bf0f" />

#### Step 11: Select your orders.
<img width="1151" height="565" alt="wasep9" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8ebb5aa9-869e-4f8b-b387-b81a1857d00e" />

#### Step 12: Click “View Air Waybill”.
<img width="617" height="875" alt="wasep10" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/21227bfd-c137-4bb3-b072-018d713e6d66" />

#### Step 13: Download and print your Air Waybill(AWB).
