# EasyParcel Shoppegram NextGen Integration - Plugin Version

#### Step 1: Login to your EasyParcel account.

#### Step 2: Click 'EasyConnect'.
![EasyParcel Shoppegram Step 1](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/step1.png)

#### Step 3: Click 'Add Ecommerce App'. Find 'Shoppegram', and then click 'Install App'.
![EasyParcel Shoppegram Step 2](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/blob/930762afaabc6fc37b74e4f09c7611c7211c898f/Pictures/Shoppegram7.png)

#### Step 4: Fill in the 'Store name' and 'Store URL'. After that, click 'Add'.
![EasyParcel Shoppegram Step 3](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/blob/930762afaabc6fc37b74e4f09c7611c7211c898f/Pictures/Shoppegram8.png)

#### Step 5: Your store API key/Integration ID will be generated. Copy it to integrate with your store.
![EasyParcel Shoppegram Step 4](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/blob/930762afaabc6fc37b74e4f09c7611c7211c898f/Pictures/Shoppegram9.png)

#### Step 6: Go to your Shoppegram admin panel, click on 'Settings', and click 'Shipping providers'.
![EasyParcel Shoppegram Step 5](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/blob/930762afaabc6fc37b74e4f09c7611c7211c898f/Pictures/Shoppegram3.png)

#### Step 7: In the Shipping provider setting, find the 'EasyParcel Delivery made easy' and click the 'Activate' button.
![EasyParcel Shoppegram Step 6](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/blob/930762afaabc6fc37b74e4f09c7611c7211c898f/Pictures/Shoppegram2.png)

#### Step 8: After that paste the API key and open the **enable EasyParcel**
![EasyParcel Shoppegram Step 7](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/blob/e5ed1ceb8bcb9fa83f1f961ad570ddcad3bc890f/Pictures/Shoppegram.png)
