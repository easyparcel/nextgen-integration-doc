# EasyParcel OrderSini NextGen Integration - Plugin Version

#### Step 1: Login to your EasyParcel account.

#### Step 2: Click 'EasyConnect'.
![EasyParcel OrderSini Step 1](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/step1.png)

#### Step 3: Click 'Add Ecommerce App'. Find the 'OrderSini'. After that, click 'Install App'.
![EasyParcel OrderSini Step 2](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/OS1.png)

#### Step 4: Fill in the 'Store name' and 'Store URL'. After that, click 'Add'.
![EasyParcel OrderSini Step 3](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/OS2.png)

#### Step 5: Your store API key/Integration ID will be generated. Copy it to integrate with your store.
![EasyParcel OrderSini Step 4](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/OS3.png)

#### Step 6: Login to your OrderSini account. Kindly go to Account Setting – Shipment Integration – click on EasyParcel logo.
![EasyParcel OrderSini Step 5](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/OS4.png)

#### Step 7: Kindly fill in API key/ Integration ID. API key/ Integration ID may get from Step 5. Every EasyParcel account has its own API key/ Integration ID.
![EasyParcel OrderSini Step 6](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/OS5.png)

#### Step 8: Kindly go to Product Setting – Order List and Click on EasyParcel button to fulfill orders.
![EasyParcel OrderSini Step 7](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/OS6.png)

#### Step 9: Click “Pay” to get the AWB from ORDERSINI platform directly.
![EasyParcel OrderSini Step 8](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/OS7.png)
