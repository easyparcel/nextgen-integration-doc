# EasyParcel SiteGiant NextGen Integration – Plugin Version

#### Step 1: Login to your EasyParcel account.

#### Step 2: Click **EasyConnect**.
![EasyParcel SiteGiant Step 1](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/step1.png)

#### Step 3: Click **Add Ecommerce App**. Search for **SiteGiant**, then click **Install App**.
![EasyParcel SiteGiant Step 2](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/siteg2.png)

#### Step 4: Fill in the **Store name** and **Store URL**, then click **Add**.
![EasyParcel SiteGiant Step 3](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/siteg3.png)

#### Step 5: Your store API key / Integration ID will be generated. Copy it to integrate with your store.
![EasyParcel SiteGiant Step 4](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/siteg4.png)

#### Step 6 – Install EasyParcel App. Go to **Apps** on the dashboard menu → **Others** → click on **EasyParcel**.
![EasyParcel SiteGiant Step 5](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/siteg5.png)

## Step 7 – Configure Contact Details
- Fill in your EasyParcel email address  
- Enter the Sender Collection address  
- Click the **Save** button
