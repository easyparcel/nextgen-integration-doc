# EasyParcel Orderla.my NextGen Integration - Plugin Version

#### Step 1: Login to your EasyParcel account.

#### Step 2: Click 'EasyConnect'.
![EasyParcel Orderla Step 2](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/step1.png)

#### Step 3: Click 'Add Ecommerce App'. Find the 'Orderla.my'. After that, click 'Install App'.
<img width="1280" height="710" alt="orderla1" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7bf40be5-dbd0-4784-8462-8aa1569b91f5" />

#### Step 4: Fill in the 'Store name' and 'Store URL'. After that, click 'Add'.
<img width="1280" height="708" alt="orderla2" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/68c94180-5025-47ff-b96c-842d035b8a4d" />

#### Step 5: Your store API key/Integration ID will be generated. Copy it to integrate with your store.
<img width="1280" height="709" alt="orderla3" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8f7894b3-3f30-456e-8bb1-237867e3119e" />

#### Step 6: Login to your Orderla.my account. Go to  Orderla's EasyParcel settings at this URL: https://orderla.my/admin/delivery-services/easyparcel and paste the API key and click 'Update Change'.
<img width="818" height="489" alt="orderla4" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/76c7206c-7d80-4369-ad1a-2ef909f3fe66" />

#### Step 7: Now you can use EasyParcel to easily purchase your parcel for shipment from Orderla system. You may also pass the data from Orderla to EasyParcel on the parcel's information easier.
##### Important: Make sure all of your products have weight.
##### Also make sure to turn on, customer detail for address.
<img width="669" height="313" alt="orderla5" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9bf54134-a287-4bc0-9757-5a4d1c80e133" />

#### Step 8: To setup the integration, first go to Form Edit / Delivery, turn on switch EasyParcel Delivery Option. 
<img width="809" height="575" alt="orderla6" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6ae0ea0c-466f-4cd2-bac8-328f613afb73" />

#### Step 9: Then setup the EasyParcel Delivery Option
<img width="793" height="663" alt="orderla7" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4211b80c-a2a9-4e65-96d5-4c8033cb7112" />

#### Step 10: At your order form, there will be an option for 'Delivery via EasyParcel'.
<img width="682" height="302" alt="orderla8" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/30f70150-8da7-4965-8e99-e694fd07267e" />

#### Step 11: After your customer proceed for 'Order Summary', it will load the list of Delivery Options that your customer might interested Then customer will click 'Select Delivery'.
<img width="590" height="636" alt="orderla9" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5d19c224-73d5-4db4-bcc6-b6a6af414e1e" />

#### Step 12: At order detail page, there is a card on 'EasyParcel Delivery', and also a button to manage it 'Manage EasyParcel Shipment'. 
<img width="590" height="636" alt="orderla10" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/384e8273-294b-4729-a267-14836db0f48b" />

#### Step 13: Make sure all the info for sender and receiver are correct, add receiver postcode and select state properly, then scroll down and click 'Purchase Parcel'.
<img width="571" height="408" alt="orderla11" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b47a543d-730c-4be7-b16c-27982f963783" />

#### Step 14: Once your purchase was success, the order detail will show the AWB link and link to track the parcel.
<img width="1000" height="294" alt="orderla12" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/50e82f0c-623a-40df-a7ef-7526a5717197" />

#### Step 15: For your customer, their order page will be updated with the link to track the parcel.
<img width="843" height="334" alt="orderla13" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9b37004e-41ff-4a1a-adb3-55e9d19b9e3f" />
