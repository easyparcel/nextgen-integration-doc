# EasyParcel Ejenkita NextGen Integration - Plugin Version

#### Step 1: Login to your EasyParcel account.

#### Step 2: Click 'EasyConnect'.
![EasyParcel EasyStore Step 2](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/step1.png)

#### Step 3: Click 'Add Ecommerce App'. Find the 'Ejenkita'. After that, click 'Install App'.
<img width="1280" height="708" alt="ejen1" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/57c9fbcf-f167-459f-8b1c-1ac7f5e335d0" />

#### Step 4: Fill in the 'Store name' and 'Store URL'. After that, click 'Add'.
<img width="1280" height="710" alt="ejen2" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4855f36d-503a-491f-828a-44e6ae3c16a4" />

#### Step 5: Your store API key/Integration ID will be generated. Copy it to integrate with your store.
<img width="1280" height="712" alt="ejen3" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b09dbf64-7fa4-4eda-b691-bd51a2c677c4" />

#### Step 6:  log in Ejenkita account and click ‘Integrate with EasyParcel’.
<img width="1061" height="584" alt="ejen4" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/32f472bb-94ce-4255-94dd-a7499ff11305" />

#### Step 7: Paste API key anda di bahagian ‘Integration ID’.
<img width="1032" height="616" alt="ejen5" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b4a765cc-775f-4972-9efd-d57c06adeab5" />
