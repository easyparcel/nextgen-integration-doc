# EasyParcel Payrecon NextGen Integration – Plugin Version

#### Step 1: Login to your EasyParcel account.

#### Step 2: Click **EasyConnect**.
![EasyParcel Payrecon Step 2](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/step1.png)

#### Step 3: Click **Add Ecommerce App**. Search for **91App**, then click **Install App**.
![EasyParcel Payrecon Step 3](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/PR1.png)

#### Step 4: Fill in the **Store name** and **Store URL**, then click **Add**.
![EasyParcel Payrecon Step 4](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/PR2.png)

#### Step 5: Your store API key / Integration ID will be generated. Copy it to integrate with your store.
![EasyParcel Payrecon Step 5](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/PR3.png)

#### Step 6: Log in to Payrecon.
![EasyParcel Payrecon Step 6](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/PR4.png)

#### Step 7: Go to Setting > User Settings. Select Other. Then click Update API.
![EasyParcel Payrecon Step 7](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/PR5.png)

#### Step 8: Paste the API Key at the respective column. Then click Update.
![EasyParcel Payrecon Step 8](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/PR6.png)

#### Step 9: Click on Set Pickup Address.
![EasyParcel Payrecon Step 9](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/PR7.png)

#### Step 10：Insert all information, and click Save Location.
![EasyParcel Payrecon Step 10](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/PR8.png)

#### Step 11: Select the order to process. Click Easy Parcel.
![EasyParcel Payrecon Step 11](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/PR9.png)

#### Step 12: Select a courier and pickup date. Then, click Book.
![EasyParcel Payrecon Step 12](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/PR10.png)

#### Step 13: A successful message will pop out if booking successful. Then click Close.
![EasyParcel Payrecon Step 13](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/PR11.png)

#### Step 14:  Tracking number has show out. Shipping label can be printed out now.
![EasyParcel Payrecon Step 14](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/PR12.png)

#### Step 15: Click Print, Print Shipping Label for Selected Orders.
![EasyParcel Payrecon Step 15](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/PR13.png)

#### Step 16:  This is the Shipping label and it is ready to print out.
![EasyParcel Payrecon Step 16](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/PR14.png)
