# EasyParcel FireSell NextGen Integration - Plugin Version

#### Step 1: Login to your EasyParcel account.

#### Step 2: Click 'EasyConnect'.
![EasyParcel FireSell Step 1](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/step1.png)

#### Step 3: Click 'Add Ecommerce App'. Find the 'FireSell'. After that, click 'Install App'.
![EasyParcel FireSell Step 1](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/fs2.png)

#### Step 4: Fill in the 'Store name' and 'Store URL'. After that, click 'Add'.
![EasyParcel FireSell Step 1](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/fs3.png)

#### Step 5: Your store API key/Integration ID will be generated. Copy it to integrate with your store.
![EasyParcel FireSell Step 1](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/fs4.png)

#### Step 6: Click  “Konfigurasi” > “ Integrasi EasyParcel” and fill in EasyParcel API Key.
![EasyParcel FireSell Step 1](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/fs5.png)
