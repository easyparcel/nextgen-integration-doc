# EasyParcel Ulive NextGen Integration - Plugin Version

#### Step 1: Login to your EasyParcel account.

#### Step 2: Click 'EasyConnect'.
![EasyParcel Ulive Step 2](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/step1.png)

#### Step 3: Click 'Add Ecommerce App'. Find the 'Ulive'. After that, click 'Install App'.
![EasyParcel Ulive Step 3](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/ulive1.png)

#### Step 4: Fill in the 'Store name' and 'Store URL'. After that, click 'Add'.
![EasyParcel Ulive Step 4](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/ulive2.png)

#### Step 5: Your store API key/Integration ID will be generated. Copy it to integrate with your store.
![EasyParcel Ulive Step 5](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/ulive3.png)

#### Step 7: Go to Setting, and next to the Tags section, you'll see EasyParcel Integration. Turn it on, paste your integration ID, and click update EasyParcel Integration to save it.
![EasyParcel Ulive Step 6](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/ulive4.png)
