# EasyParcel TikTok Shop Integration

---

## Introduction
This guide will walk you through integrating NextGen EasyParcel with TikTok Shop. With this integration, you'll import orders from TikTok Shop into your EasyParcel account for fulfillment.

---


### Integrate your TikTok Shop store in EasyParcel website
**Step 1:** [Log in to your EasyParcel account](https://account.easyparcel.com/login?client_id=c575e8cd-aa46-46db-8308-e18d25bb76c6&redirect_uri=https%3A%2F%2Fapp.easyparcel.com%2Feasyaccount%2Fcallback&state=eyJjbGllbnRfaWQiOiI1M2FmYmQzMS05OGI2LTQ3ODctOWYzOC1kMDY5ZGRkN2RiM2QiLCJyZWRpcmVjdF91cmkiOiJodHRwczovL2FwcC5lYXN5cGFyY2VsLmNvbS9sb2dpbi9vYXV0aC9jYWxsYmFjayIsInN0YXRlIjoie30iLCJjb3VudHJ5IjoibXkiLCJsYW5nIjoiZW4ifQ%3D%3D&country=my)

**Step 2:** After login, click on **Add Ecommerce App** under **EasyConnect** section, find **TikTok Shop** and click on **Install App**

**Step 3:** Click **Allow Access** to authorize EasyParcel to access your TikTok Shop store data
<img width="853" height="445" alt="Screenshot 2026-02-05 at 11 17 15 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/006a291a-d5ab-4207-b4d0-fd4340a7cb6e" />


**Step 4:** Enter your store name, store url and shop id, then click **Connect**
<img width="906" height="606" alt="Screenshot 2026-02-05 at 11 19 54 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7dba3009-6d20-44eb-8b80-59cbdbe56ee2" />


**Step 5:** You will be redirected to TikTok Shop Seller Centre to authorize the app. First, select the region of the seller account by clicking **Next**
<img width="1440" height="714" alt="Screenshot 2026-02-05 at 11 23 10 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/eda7920e-e5fc-493f-9708-cc07e5f88711" />

**Step 6:** Ensure the authorized shop is the shop you wish to connect. Select partner access duration based on your preferences and enter the contact email address. Finally, click **Confirm to install**
<img width="1440" height="714" alt="Screenshot 2026-02-05 at 11 32 52 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ee3a26c6-57a2-414a-b67c-05cec321d560" />

**Step 7:** Acknowledge the authorization scopes by checking the acknowledge message, then click **Authorise** 
<img width="1440" height="715" alt="Screenshot 2026-02-05 at 11 33 56 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/bfa0971b-4fc5-4264-911c-89367ff883fa" />

**Step 8:** Then you will be redirected back to EasyParcel website in 5 seconds, you may also click **Done** button to redirect immediately

### Setup Integrated App
**Step 1:** Under **EasyConnect**, click on **Installed Ecommerce Apps**, find **TikTok Shop** you may find your Wix app successfully install.
<img width="1405" height="522" alt="Screenshot 2026-02-05 at 11 56 47 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ad9b3978-8862-463e-9412-097cdf0f7aca" />



**Step 2:** In the store, click **Sender Address** and key in your sender details and click on the **Update** button.
<img width="1387" height="461" alt="Screenshot 2026-01-26 at 9 52 18 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7980f34e-6fa1-4073-a599-fc823753a84e" />
