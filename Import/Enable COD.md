# How to enable COD 
## Single Fulfillment
1. On the single fulfillment page, if the courier selected supports COD, COD will appear below the Add-on Services section. 
<img width="1091" height="441" alt="Screenshot 2025-11-27 at 9 41 19 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/95227126-5bcb-4960-8c1a-b5eb18dc7af2" />

2. When the Enable button is clicked, a modal is displayed to allow user fill in COD amount
<img width="1117" height="464" alt="Screenshot 2025-11-27 at 9 43 10 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4e7ff5bc-2707-4396-a447-34b1dd58b2bd" />

3. If a user inserts invalid value such as empty value, 0, value that not within range, error message will shown below the Amount field.
<img width="1120" height="461" alt="Screenshot 2025-11-27 at 9 44 01 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d2038b72-c081-485e-bdf9-f96c0bd9b4bf" />
<img width="1124" height="468" alt="Screenshot 2025-11-27 at 9 44 19 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/05c7e3b4-599b-489c-ae1b-08b9073261c4" />

---

4. Users are not allowed to save the changes when there is error on the COD value
<img width="1121" height="463" alt="Screenshot 2025-11-27 at 9 44 57 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b6938863-b784-49b5-afff-d47e747db3a3" />

5. When users hovered on the (i) icon, a tooptip displayed to show COD details (total amount, charges, amount receive)
<img width="1116" height="471" alt="Screenshot 2025-11-27 at 9 45 28 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1aa2ce1f-eee8-4220-8598-20eaf9af5a4c" />

6. Once users click the Save button, the COD is enabled and details will shown below COD section
<img width="1094" height="463" alt="Screenshot 2025-11-27 at 9 46 03 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d375f48d-37c5-4be7-a3ff-5f1bb0563bc0" />


## Bulk Fulfillment
1. When users open edit modal on an order, the COD section will be displayed in the Add-On Services section if it is available for the selected courier
<img width="1117" height="465" alt="Screenshot 2025-11-27 at 9 30 12 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/52f2f28c-59a1-4e53-80a1-4593f4760da9" />

2. Users can enable it by choosing "Yes" from Enable select list. When "Yes"is chosen, the Amount field can be inserted
