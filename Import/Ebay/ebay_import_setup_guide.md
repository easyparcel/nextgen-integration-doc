# EasyParcel Ebay Integration

---

## Introduction
This guide will walk you through integrating NextGen EasyParcel with Ebay. With this integration, you'll import orders from Ebay into your EasyParcel account for fulfillment.

---

### Setup Integrated App
**Step 1:** Under **EasyConnect**, click on **Installed Ecommerce Apps**, find **Ebay** you may find your Ebay app successfully install.
<img width="1440" height="717" alt="Screenshot 2026-03-13 at 10 28 43 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/eb401e4e-c7d6-4645-a6e7-b227421d9105" />


**Step 2:** In the store, click **Sender Address** and key in your sender details and click on the **Update** button.
<img width="1373" height="460" alt="Screenshot 2026-03-13 at 10 29 52 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/18285c46-709a-4499-8dc0-312bfedea318" />


**Done ! You have successfully setup Ebay app in easyparcel.**
