## Integration Steps

1. **Install the App**
   - In the Next Gen Portal, navigate to 'EasyConnect → Add Ecommerce App`.
   - Find the `CubeCart` app and click **Install App**.
   
   
   <img width="1439" height="710" alt="Screenshot 2026-02-24 at 8 36 10 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d6b2c81f-74dd-4047-9f4e-2fbc4ee5a712" />



2. **Grant Access**
   - Click **Allow Access** when prompted.
   
   <img width="1440" height="714" alt="Screenshot 2026-02-24 at 8 38 56 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4a0e1a69-d3a4-4ebb-b2fb-08f8d187b505" />




3. **Connect Your Store**
   - Enter your store name and click **Next**.
   
    <img width="1438" height="713" alt="Screenshot 2026-02-24 at 8 40 47 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1a160cfd-f615-4c5e-b499-9dd77ba3606f" />



4. **Upload Integration Kit**
   - Download the integration kit provided.
   - Upload the kit to your CubeCart system folder.
   - Enter the integration kit URL and click **SUBMIT**.
