# Set the product english name on Shopify

#### Step 1: Login to your Shopify.

#### Step 2: Click 'Settings'.
<img width="1271" height="639" alt="Screenshot 2026-02-25 at 9 58 24 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7f427bae-6595-499d-8f00-78cce2070b8c" />

#### Step 3: Click 'Metafields and metaobjects'. Then, click 'Variants'.
<img width="1270" height="628" alt="Screenshot 2026-02-25 at 9 59 34 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9c8b578c-112b-4979-b049-68a1723fe4de" />

#### Step 4: After that, click 'Add Definition'.
<img width="1274" height="650" alt="Screenshot 2026-02-25 at 10 04 13 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a8b8f0f7-6f97-4417-a8b2-fe6cc72cd476" />

#### Step 5: Enter 'english_name' in the Name field and select 'Single Line Text' as the type.
<img width="1109" height="646" alt="Screenshot 2026-04-14 at 10 12 53 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/fdad3c86-1cf5-44da-a937-c90be90ff780" />

#### Step 6: Click 'Save'. 
<img width="1085" height="716" alt="Screenshot 2026-04-14 at 10 13 52 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f62adced-e05e-4d79-9dfe-c20c384e9430" />

#### Step 7: Go back to home page and go to 'Products'. Select a specific item in order to set its english name by entering values in the english_name field that created just now.
<img width="1440" height="452" alt="Screenshot 2026-04-14 at 10 18 00 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c5871382-d048-4bb1-9077-91a7ebf1a711" />


#### Step 8: Scroll down until you find the 'Variant metafields' section. Please fill in the 'english_name' field.
<img width="1019" height="459" alt="Screenshot 2026-04-14 at 10 17 09 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/82e9acf1-4274-415f-b63c-2c549be6052d" />

#### Step 9: After filling all fields, save them so that EasyParcel can fetch the item name correctly.
<img width="1334" height="663" alt="Screenshot 2026-04-14 at 10 19 52 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/31762f0d-1112-4afc-bf77-9ec429985a2a" />

#### Note: EasyParcel retrieves the item name using the following priority:
1. Variant metafield
2. Product metafield
3. Original item name (fallback if no metafield is found)
