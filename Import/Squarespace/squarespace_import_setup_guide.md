# EasyParcel Squarespace Integration

---

## Introduction
This guide will walk you through integrating NextGen EasyParcel with Squarespace. With this integration, you'll import orders from Squarespace into your EasyParcel account for fulfillment.

---


### Integrate your Squarespace store in EasyParcel website
**Step 1:** [Log in to your EasyParcel account](https://account.easyparcel.com/login?client_id=c575e8cd-aa46-46db-8308-e18d25bb76c6&redirect_uri=https%3A%2F%2Fapp.easyparcel.com%2Feasyaccount%2Fcallback&state=eyJjbGllbnRfaWQiOiI1M2FmYmQzMS05OGI2LTQ3ODctOWYzOC1kMDY5ZGRkN2RiM2QiLCJyZWRpcmVjdF91cmkiOiJodHRwczovL2FwcC5lYXN5cGFyY2VsLmNvbS9sb2dpbi9vYXV0aC9jYWxsYmFjayIsInN0YXRlIjoie30iLCJjb3VudHJ5IjoibXkiLCJsYW5nIjoiZW4ifQ%3D%3D&country=my)

**Step 2:** After login, click on **Add Ecommerce App** under **Integration** section, find **Squarespace** and click on **Install App**

**Step 3:** Click **Allow Access** to authorize EasyParcel to access your Squarespace store data
<img width="100%" alt="Screenshot 2026-05-12 at 10 51 11 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/44f7c720-a148-4983-8134-4035f3965eb5" />


**Step 4:** Enter your store name and store url, then click **Connect**

**Step 5:** You will be redirected to Squarespace to grant app permissions. Click **Allow** to grant easyparcel the permissions

### Setup Integrated App
**Step 1:** Under **Integration**, click on **Installed Ecommerce Apps**, find **Squarespace** you may find your Squarespace app successfully install.
<img width="100%" alt="Screenshot 2026-05-12 at 10 56 10 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b77fa945-81ff-4a15-a25a-839fc9687ec9" />


**Step 2:** In the store, click **Sender Address** and key in your sender details and click on the **Update** button.
<img width="1373" height="460" alt="Screenshot 2026-03-13 at 10 29 52 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/18285c46-709a-4499-8dc0-312bfedea318" />


**Done ! You have successfully setup Squarespace app in easyparcel.**

Visit [Fulfillment steps](https://easyparcel.github.io/nextgen-integration-doc/Import/Squarespace/squarespace_import_fulfilment) guide to start the fulfillment process.
