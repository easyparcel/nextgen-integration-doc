# How to merge order for imported orders
1. Bulk select order that you want to fulfill, then click **Fulfill Selected** button
   <img width="100%" alt="Screenshot 2026-04-13 at 5 37 57 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0ca59e0e-c480-425c-b3b7-11a1adf3770d" />

## Merge all orders with same address
2. In the bulk fulfillment page, click **Merge all orders with same address** button if you want to combine the orders that having same receiver address into same order
    <img width="100%" alt="Screenshot 2026-04-13 at 5 41 14 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/aa897d1e-cc26-4dba-99ed-767e8676b240" />
    

3. After that, the orders with same receiver address will be merged and the courier rates will be recalculated for the merged order
    <img width="100%" alt="Screenshot 2026-04-13 at 5 45 51 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/166b9be4-e9b5-4b48-b0a9-ba6b406e6661" />
    
## Merge only selected order
4. If you want to merge only specific order, select the orders you want to merge then click **Merge selected**
   <img width="100%" alt="Screenshot 2026-04-13 at 5 50 15 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4a69979b-aa55-4633-a9ae-f7359fc96d95" />

5. After that, the orders selected with same receiver address will be merged and the courier rates will be recalculated for the merged order
    <img width="100%" alt="Screenshot 2026-04-13 at 5 45 51 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/166b9be4-e9b5-4b48-b0a9-ba6b406e6661" />

## Undo Merge
6. If you want to undo the merged order, just click the undo merge icon on the merge oder
   <img width="100%" alt="Screenshot 2026-04-13 at 5 53 39 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/932f87e1-ad8c-460c-b42b-990e7e3ac918" />

## Auto Merge in Bulk Fulfillment Page
7. If you want the system to automatically merge the orders with same receiver address when you visit bulk fulfillment page, you may enable **Auto Merge Order in Bulk Fulfillment** setting in Settings page
   <img width="100%" alt="Screenshot 2026-04-13 at 5 57 46 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/09fb1596-8ad7-475e-9f4d-33f43b00547e" />

8. After you enable it, when you visit bulk fulfillment page, order with same receiver address will be automatically merged into same order
   <img width="100%"  alt="Screenshot 2026-04-13 at 6 00 24 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4c941cda-3eb2-40d7-a21b-eca152167c89" />
