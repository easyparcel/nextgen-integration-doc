# EasyParcel Amazon Integration

---

## Introduction
This guide will walk you through integrating NextGen EasyParcel with Amazon. With this integration, you'll import orders from Amazon into your EasyParcel account for fulfillment.

---

**Step 2:** After login, click on **Add Ecommerce App** under **Integration** section, find **Amazon** and click on **Install App**

## Setup Integrated App
**Step 1:** Under **Integration**, click on **Installed Ecommerce Apps**, find **Amazon** you may find your Wix app successfully install.
<img width="100%" alt="Screenshot 2026-04-13 at 9 56 13 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/57a7c3de-18ab-417f-b314-4b223ff92cad" />



**Step 2:** In the store, click **Sender Address** and key in your sender details and click on the **Update** button.
<img width="100%" alt="Screenshot 2026-01-26 at 9 52 18 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7980f34e-6fa1-4073-a599-fc823753a84e" />


**Done ! You have successfully setup Amazon app in easyparcel.**
