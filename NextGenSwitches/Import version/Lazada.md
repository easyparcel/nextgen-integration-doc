# Lazada Migration
To migrate your Classic EasyParcel import version integration with your Lazada Store to NextGen version. You need to follow this file for the setup steps.

### Navigation path:        
**Login your EasyParcel Account -> Integration -> Installed Ecommerce Apps -> Lazada Import -> Open App -> Select your Store**
<img width="1280" height="800" alt="Screenshot 2026-03-12 at 5 24 42 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/06a04c8d-6e80-4bd5-a894-82c5cf1d331c" />


You will be directed to this page. After that, click the **Order** button.
<img width="1010" height="576" alt="Screenshot 2026-03-12 at 4 19 24 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e2a22d02-f74f-47cf-9fda-943e24f5c42a" />

Click the re-authorize button, to re-authorize your Import version integration from Classic to NextGen.
<img width="1016" height="601" alt="Screenshot 2026-03-12 at 5 21 28 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0a213c7e-e73c-4b9b-bbd6-b792897a72e2" />
