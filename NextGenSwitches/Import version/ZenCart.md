# ZenCart Migration
To migrate your EasyParcel import version integration with your ZenCart from Classic to NextGen version, you may refer to this file for the setup steps.

🧭 Navigation path:        
**Login your EasyParcel Account -> Integration -> Installed Ecommerce Apps -> Cube Cart -> Open App -> Select your Store**
<img width="1280" height="800" alt="Screenshot 2026-03-12 at 5 29 34 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2cdf3bcd-7d66-48ff-a0a4-09b8dff7dc0c" />

You will be directed to this page. After that, click the **Order** button.
<img width="1005" height="563" alt="Screenshot 2026-03-12 at 3 24 42 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/21960841-a954-40c2-b165-193b7d508118" />
📝 Note: You will found that there have a message show in this page, **Legacy Integration Kit Detected**.


Click to the message, and click the reauthorize button
<img width="1000" height="561" alt="Screenshot 2026-03-12 at 3 41 27 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2fa9a67c-7652-4779-953b-ca450913e6a8" />

**Upload Integration Kit**
   - Download the integration kit provided.
   - Upload the kit to your ZenCart system folder.
   - Enter the integration kit URL and click **SUBMIT**.
<img width="1440" height="715" alt="Screenshot 2026-02-24 at 8 51 18 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9f7ddef7-87bf-4bc1-b14f-426e8bbee125" />


### Migration Done✅, you are able to import your ZenCart Order into your EasyParcel NextGen Account.
