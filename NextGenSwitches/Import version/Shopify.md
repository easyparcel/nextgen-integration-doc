# Shopify Migration
To migrate your Classic EasyParcel import version integration with your Shopify Store to NextGen version. You need to follow this file for the setup steps.

### Navigation path:        
**Login your EasyParcel Account -> Integration -> Installed Ecommerce Apps -> Shopify Import -> Open App -> Select your Store**
<img width="1280" height="800" alt="Screenshot 2026-03-12 at 5 16 39 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/bb921d23-97ed-4de9-a67b-ef7b92c7eb79" />

You will be directed to this page. After that, click the **Order** button.
<img width="1016" height="597" alt="Screenshot 2026-03-12 at 2 58 17 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6cdd4999-59ad-4471-8ac2-a0186fbba3e2" />

Click the re-authorize button, to re-authorize your Import version integration from Classic to NextGen.
<img width="1006" height="590" alt="Screenshot 2026-03-12 at 5 18 55 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/94f84870-c6a4-48e6-a7fc-23229c6dfe90" />

Click the install button.          
<img width="651" height="511" alt="Screenshot 2026-03-12 at 3 02 04 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/400d9e20-c9eb-4da2-af19-cdfec847a411" />

### Migration Done✅, you are able to import your Shopify Order into your EasyParcel NextGen Account.
