# Welcome to Becoming a NextGen User

This guide explains how to switch your e-commerce store integration from the Classic version (older version) to the NextGen version (latest version).

## What Happens After You Switch to NextGen

- Orders will be recorded in your NextGen account  
- You will be able to use the latest system enhancements  
- Improved and faster performance  

## What Happens If You Do Not Switch

- Orders will continue to be recorded in your Classic account  
- You will not be able to review orders in your EasyParcel NextGen account  
- Slower system performance
