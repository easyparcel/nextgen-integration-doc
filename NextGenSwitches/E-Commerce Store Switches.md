# Switching from Classic to NextGen for your e-commerce store integration

## Navigation Path
#### Login to EasyParcel website -> EasyConnect -> Installed Ecommerce Apps -> Select your ecommerce store -> Open App

---
## Steps to Switch
1. Go to the **Version** section.  
2. Toggle the switch from **Classic** to **Next Gen**.
3. Press the **Save button**
<img width="1280" height="701" alt="image" src="https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/blob/6ed5f937fac29052efef52b6c997de2b1d457be3/Pictures/General2.png" />
