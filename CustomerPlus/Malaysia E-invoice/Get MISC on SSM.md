# how to get your business MSIC on your SSM 🔐
## What is business MISC 📊?
A business MSIC code (🔗[Malaysia Standard Industrial Classification](https://sdk.myinvois.hasil.gov.my/codes/msic-codes/)) is a 5-digit code used to classify business activities, mandatory for SSM registration and e-invoicing.

## How to get it
First, Sign up or Login your SSM account.
<img width="911" height="782" alt="img_v3_0210d_c69056cc-9954-4c64-8c69-c9950c7462hu" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a878d072-c5c0-40ed-8f28-8b194addbff3" />

Second, Click the **"My Business Services"** -> **"Registration Status/History"**.
<img width="1222" height="782" alt="img_v3_0210d_d4ab8e5c-80fa-473e-8770-3a80c73b63hu" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/170db002-1d3a-4d38-8e90-465a8b7478bc" />

Third, Click the **"Approve/Reject"** -> **Click the action button**📝
