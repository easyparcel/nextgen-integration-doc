# EasyParcel EzBoxup NextGen Integration – Plugin Version

#### Step 1: Login to your EasyParcel account.

#### Step 2: Click 'EasyConnect'.
![EasyParcel EzBoxup Step 1](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/step1.png)

#### Step 3: Click 'Add Ecommerce App'. Find the 'EzBoxup'. After that, click 'Install App'.
![EasyParcel EzBoxup Step 2](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/ez2.png)

#### Step 4: Fill in the 'Store name' and 'Store URL'. After that, click 'Add'.
![EasyParcel EzBoxup Step 3](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/ez3.png)

#### Step 5: Your store API key/Integration ID will be generated. Copy it to integrate with your store.
![EasyParcel EzBoxup Step 4](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/ez4.png)

#### Step 6: In Ezboxup, kindly go to Delivery Option >  Easy Parcel
![EasyParcel EzBoxup Step 5](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/ez5.png)

#### Step 7: Click to enable EasyParcel, fill in Integration ID, choose courier preference and click “Save Setting”. Integration ID may get from Step 5.
![EasyParcel EzBoxup Step 6](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/ez6.png)

#### Step 8: To ship your order, kindly go to Order Status > Shipping
![EasyParcel EzBoxup Step 7](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/ez7.png)

#### Step 9: Click “EasyParcel” to proceed.
![EasyParcel EzBoxup Step 8](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/ez8.png)

#### Step 10: Fill in all the shipping details and submit by clicking “Confirm Shipping”.
![EasyParcel EzBoxup Step 9](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/ez9.png)
