# EasyParcel Kuasa NextGen Integration - Plugin Version

#### Step 1: Login to your EasyParcel account.

#### Step 2: Click 'EasyConnect'.
![EasyParcel EasyStore Step 1](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/step1.png)

#### Step 3: Click 'Add Ecommerce App'. Find the 'Kuasa'. After that, click 'Install App'.
<img width="1280" height="703" alt="kuasa1" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d5b38142-569d-4345-a8ab-6ffd377c2d58" />

#### Step 4: Fill in the 'Store name' and 'Store URL'. After that, click 'Add'.
<img width="1280" height="711" alt="kuasa2" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5df1f878-341d-4f0e-beea-1fe650a36ad4" />

#### Step 5: Your store API key/Integration ID will be generated. Copy it to integrate with your store.
<img width="1272" height="711" alt="kuasa3" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/57e2f66f-1414-44bb-b13c-19d4b781b02e" />

#### Step 6: Login ke akaun anda melalui https://sales.kuasa.io/. Then, Klik Settings.
<img width="1222" height="653" alt="kuasa4" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/173faadd-d245-42d8-9c81-fd9304c10efa" />

#### Step 7: Klik Shipping Method.
<img width="1233" height="654" alt="kuasa5" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2a64061a-2b6f-465c-b1e9-875cd93be676" />

#### Step 8: Pastikan anda berada di menu EasyParcel dan klik pada kurier yang anda gunakan.
<img width="981" height="546" alt="kuasa6" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/93149d75-e512-405b-91bd-8cceccd98316" />

#### Step 9: Isikan Integration ID daripada EasyParcel dan pastikan toggle bar menunjukkan ON, dan klik Update.
<img width="1221" height="650" alt="kuasa7" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/40da52ec-cf49-4c77-ad93-d4875fcf1891" />
