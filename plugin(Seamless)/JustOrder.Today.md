# EasyParcel JustOrder.Today NextGen Integration - Plugin Version

#### Step 1: Login to your EasyParcel account.

#### Step 2: Click 'EasyConnect'.
![EasyParcel JustOrder.Today Step 2](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/step1.png)

#### Step 3: Click 'Add Ecommerce App'. Find the 'JustOrder.Today'. After that, click 'Install App'.
![EasyParcel JustOrder.Today Step 3](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/jot1.png)

#### Step 4: Fill in the 'Store name' and 'Store URL'. After that, click 'Add'.
![EasyParcel JustOrder.Today Step 4](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/jot2.png)

#### Step 5: Your store API key/Integration ID will be generated. Copy it to integrate with your store.
![EasyParcel JustOrder.Today Step 5](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/jot3.png)

#### Step 6: Login JustOrder.Today account > click on your shop.
![EasyParcel JustOrder.Today Step 6](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/jot4.png)

#### Step 7: Click “Shipments”.
![EasyParcel JustOrder.Today Step 7](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/jot5.png)

#### Step 8: In the “EasyParcel” tab, check the “Enable EasyParcel” checkbox and fill up all the details. Key in your API Key/Integration ID. Click “Save”.
![EasyParcel JustOrder.Today Step 8](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/jot6.png)
