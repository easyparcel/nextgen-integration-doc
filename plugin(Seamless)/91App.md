# EasyParcel 91App NextGen Integration – Plugin Version

#### Step 1: Login to your EasyParcel account.

#### Step 2: Click **EasyConnect**.
![EasyParcel 91App Step 1](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/step1.png)

#### Step 3: Click **Add Ecommerce App**. Search for **91App**, then click **Install App**.
![EasyParcel 91App Step 2](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/91.png)

#### Step 4: Fill in the **Store name** and **Store URL**, then click **Add**.
![EasyParcel 91App Step 3](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/92.png)

#### Step 5: Your store API key / Integration ID will be generated. Copy it to integrate with your store.
![EasyParcel 91App Step 4](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/93.png)

#### Step 6: Login to your 91APP account >click on “Store” > “Payment & Shipping” > “EasyParcel Service”. Key in your API Key/Integration ID.
![EasyParcel 91App Step 5](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/95.png)

#### Step 7: Once activated, you can go to “Orders” > “Shipping”. Select order you want to process and click on the “Shipping” button.
![EasyParcel 91App Step 6](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/96.png)

#### Step 8: Make sure your Shipping Method is “Ship with EasyParcel” and with completed shipping details before booking.
![EasyParcel 91App Step 7](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/97.png)

#### Step 9: A list of courier option will be given to choose from. Select your preferred courier and click “Fulfill”.
![EasyParcel 91App Step 8](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/98.png)

#### Step 10: Once the booking’s successfully made, click on “Print Waybill”.
![EasyParcel 91App Step 9](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/99.png)
