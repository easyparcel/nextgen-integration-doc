# EasyParcel Yezza NextGen Integration – Plugin Version

#### Step 1: Login to your EasyParcel account.

#### Step 2: Click **EasyConnect**.
![EasyParcel Yezza Step 2](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/step1.png)

#### Step 3: Click **Add Ecommerce App**. Search for **Yezza**, then click **Install App**.
![EasyParcel Yezza Step 3](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/yezza1.png)

#### Step 4: Fill in the **Store name** and **Store URL**, then click **Add**.
![EasyParcel Yezza Step 4](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/yezza2.png)

#### Step 5: Your store API key / Integration ID will be generated. Copy it to integrate with your store.
![EasyParcel Yezza Step 5](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/yezza3.png)

#### Step 6: Kindly go to Yezza platform – dashboard – Setting – EasyParcel.
![EasyParcel Yezza Step 6](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/yezza4.png)

#### Step 7: Set up your details here such as Signup EasyParcel account, Set product weight and activate the process.
![EasyParcel Yezza Step 7](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/yezza5.png)

#### Step 8: Fill in your integration ID /API key. May get the API Key/ Integration ID from Step 5.
![EasyParcel Yezza Step 8](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/yezza6.png)

#### Step 9: Set your shipment preference.
![EasyParcel Yezza Step 9](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/yezza7.png)

#### Step 10： choose prefered courier then click on Next Button.
![EasyParcel Yezza Step 10](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/yezza8.png)
