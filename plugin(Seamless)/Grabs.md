# EasyParcel Grabs NextGen Integration – Plugin Version

#### Step 1: Login to your EasyParcel account.

#### Step 2: Click **EasyConnect**.
![EasyParcel Grabs Step 2](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/step1.png)

#### Step 3: Click **Add Ecommerce App**. Search for **91App**, then click **Install App**.
![EasyParcel Grabs Step 3](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/grabs1.png)

#### Step 4: Fill in the **Store name** and **Store URL**, then click **Add**.
![EasyParcel Grabs Step 4](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/grabs2.png)

#### Step 5: Your store API key / Integration ID will be generated. Copy it to integrate with your store.
![EasyParcel Grabs Step 5](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/grabs3.png)

#### Step 6: Go to My account.
![EasyParcel Grabs Step 6](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/grabs4.png)

#### Step 7: Scroll down to marketplace section  and click “EasyParcel”.
![EasyParcel Grabs Step 7](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/grabs5.png)

#### Step 8: Fill Up Details.
![EasyParcel Grabs Step 8](https://github.com/easyparcel/nextgen-integration-doc/raw/main/Pictures/grabs6.png)
