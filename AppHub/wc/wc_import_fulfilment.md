# How do I import and fulfil my Woocommerce orders by using EasyParcel in next Gen?

This guide will walk you through Orders Fulfilment NextGen EasyParcel with Woocommerce using the **Import Version** method. With this integration, you'll import orders from Woocommerce into your EasyParcel account for fulfillment.

---

## Steps to import and fulfil orders using Woocommerce Import method

#### **Step 1:** Under "EasyConnect", click on "Installed Ecommerce Apps", find "WooCommerce Import" and click on "Open App" button.

<img width="1280" height="636" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0fc59b86-96aa-4f61-aef1-94f543acaed9" />

#### **Step 2:** Find your connected store on the store page, and click into the store.

<img width="1280" height="657" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e6d122bd-39e7-48de-9667-44f153a7535c" />

#### **Step 3:** Click on "Order", select the order date and order status and click on the "Search" button. The order will be imported and shown in the list below. Then, click the "Fulfill" button on the order you want to fulfill.

<img width="1280" height="678" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/81c5fe04-c70c-416b-b22d-2cdb682ce0e3" />


#### **Step 4:** After clicking the "Fulfill" button, you can find your order details and click on the "Get Quote" button.

<img width="1280" height="735" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/331a4257-0a50-4758-ace9-6eca6da92d56" />


#### **Step 5:**  Choose the preferred courier option and click on the "Proceed to Payment" button.

<img width="1280" height="664" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/323eecfe-08f5-45de-8e47-c10580dfb4bb" />


#### **Step 6:** The order status, order number, shipment number, and price will be displayed. Click on the "View Orders" button.

<img width="1280" height="654" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b42535e8-3c57-4a4a-b079-6d15d96ef312" />

#### **Step 6:**  The fulfilled order will now be displayed on the Order Page.

<img width="1280" height="665" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/917d1b84-a195-4846-8585-4d5bbc30ca3b" />

#### **Step 7:**  Click on the "Details" button. Order details will be displayed on the page.

<img width="1280" height="671" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c348fd38-8b85-46cb-a2aa-bff4a14c4d95" />

#### Done! You have successfully fulfill and order via woocommerce import.
