## Integration Steps

1. **Install the App**
   - In the Next Gen Portal, navigate to 'EasyConnect → Add Ecommerce App`.
   - Find the `ZenCart` app and click **Install App**.
   
   <img width="1440" height="713" alt="Screenshot 2026-02-24 at 8 48 22 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8a9a435b-920c-4d80-8274-c00b6242ad2b" />

   



2. **Grant Access**
   - Click **Allow Access** when prompted.
   
   <img width="1439" height="707" alt="Screenshot 2026-02-24 at 8 49 35 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2d3ac34b-f088-4a3a-beaa-bd7ff43c1cf8" />





3. **Connect Your Store**
   - Enter your store name and click **Next**.
     
     <img width="1440" height="716" alt="Screenshot 2026-02-24 at 8 50 21 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/02836f85-1206-4821-99e2-62451622e07c" />




4. **Upload Integration Kit**
   - Download the integration kit provided.
   - Upload the kit to your ZenCart system folder.
   - Enter the integration kit URL and click **SUBMIT**.
